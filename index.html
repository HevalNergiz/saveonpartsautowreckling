This is who we are.

The Save On Parts team is more than a group of vehicle enthusiasts who know their way around a toolbox. We have been in business for more than 50 years, and in that time, we have worked hard to become the best used auto parts source in the area. Our scrapyard does more than act as a retirement home for broken down and damaged cars and trucks. We help all usable parts have a new life with you. At the same time, we help keep your vehicle on the road for less.

FAQ

Check out the answers to some of the most common questions we get. If you need more information, don’t hesitate to contact us at any time during business hours.

Our scrapyard is full of cars, trucks, SUVs, and other vehicles from a variety of sources. Mostly, they come from people just like you who either had a total car breakdown or an accident that totaled their ride. We get the privilege of recycling them and giving parts new life. Do you have a vehicle you want to get rid of quickly? Give us a call.

What kind of parts do you need? We have a vast and ever-changing selection to choose from. Rest assured that each one is carefully inspected to make sure it is the best quality possible. Your best bet is to contact us and ask for information about your specific needs.

There are many reasons! The most important ones for many people are sustainability and cost. We recycle cars here at Save On Parts and make sure usable parts don’t end up thrown away. That’s better for the Earth. Also, quality used parts cost a lot less than buying new.

We’re friendly, convenient, dedicated to a job well done, and have a strong track record to prove it. Since Save On Parts is the largest scrapyard in the area, you will find the biggest selection of parts on hand with more becoming available every day. Also, we offer a 90-day warranty on every vehicle part sold here.

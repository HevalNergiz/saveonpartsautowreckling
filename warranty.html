Warranty

Not every used parts seller offers a guarantee on every sale, but we do. Save On Parts offers a 90-day warranty on all parts. Not only does this help you make the right decision when sourcing what you need to fix up your vehicle, it helps us keep our excellent reputation. What does this mean for you? If you come down to our location or otherwise arrange the purchase of one of our recycled parts, you get the best quality around. If something goes wrong with that part within 90 days of purchase, contact us for return and refund information. It’s as easy as that.
